Wishwash Policy

1- Customers must check and count their items thoroughly before handing in them to the delivery agent and advise if they have any comments.

2- Customers must check their items thoroughly upon receipt from the delivery agent and confirm acceptance. If the customers are not happy with the service, Wishwash will re-launder the items free of charge. However, if the customer confirms his acceptance, Wishwash won’t be held responsible afterwards to any damage that may occur, nor will they re-launder the items in question for free.

3- Wishwash guarantees garment shape maintenance, color grade, and cleanliness 95% of the time. If accidents where to occur for any force majeure, Wishwash will reimburse the customer with 70% of the item’s current market price, as long as the customer has an invoice or a proof of purchase. Otherwise, Wishwash will estimate the value based upon their own brand experts. And in eitherways, Wishwash will be entitled to keep the item in question for examination after reimbursement.

4- Delivery and pick-up service are not for free and estimated at 30 -35 EGP in total.

5- Minimum order value is 80 EGP.

6- Packages: If the weight of the order exceeds the designated weight tiers; 6KG, 9KG, 12KG, or 20KG by as much as 200grams, package will automatically get upgraded to the following tier.

7- Wishwash pick-up/delivery agent’s designated waiting time at the door step is 10 mins.

8- If a customer wishes to re-schedule the pick-up or the delivery after the arrival of the agent, re-scheduling must be done through the app and not through the agent.
